Max's Film Picks

Home or Cinema?

Are you an at-home cinema type of person, utilising Netflix, Disney+ or Amazon Prime? Or do you enjoy the Big Screen Cinema experience? Personally, I enjoy the Big Screen Cinema experience for the big films that come out. Think along the lines of Dunkirk or Star Wars. However, less influential films are enjoyed at home in the comfort of my own home with my girlfriend and dog.

My Film Picks:

Hot Fuzz: one of my all-time favourites. I can watch it again and again.

Fantastic Mr. Fox: Wes Anderson masterpiece. Easy watching and really fun.

Batman - The Dark Knight: what's not to love?
